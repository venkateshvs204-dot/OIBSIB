About The Legend

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and the 11th President of India. His contributions to aerospace engineering, missile technology, education, and youth inspiration continue to influence millions around the world.

Major Achievements

🚀 Missile Program

Led India's Integrated Guided Missile Development Program.

🏅 Bharat Ratna

Received India's highest civilian award in 1997.

🇮🇳 President

Served as the 11th President of India from 2002–2007.

📚 Author

Wrote inspirational books including Wings of Fire.

Life Timeline

1931

Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

1980

Successfully launched India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle.

1997

Awarded Bharat Ratna.

2002

Became President of India.

2015

Passed away while inspiring students during a lecture.
